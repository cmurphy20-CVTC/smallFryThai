About Us

We are three friends who graduated from culinary school together. One day we had the idea to open our own food truck. However, we faced a problem that every graduates faces, we were broke. So we saved up our money and eventually were able to open our own truck here in the Chippewa Valley!

One thing the three of us could agree on is to serve Thai food becuase it's That food! We loved these dishes and have a passion for making them. Don't let our name fool you our dish pack a lot of flavor! We hope to see you at one of our events this summer!
